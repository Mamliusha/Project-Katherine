About Katherine


As a classically trained cellist, Jennifer began learning cello at the age of eight. She completed her music studies in the Jose White Music Conservatory in Cuba and further developed her technique under the tuition of Amanda Truelove and Alexander Boyarsky, both prestigious cello professors at the Royal College of Music in London.
over 10 years of experience in solo, chamber and orchestral performances, Jennifer is able to provide bespoke entertainment for weddings, corporate events and parties. She has taken part in a wide variety of exciting projects, including recordings at Abbey Road Studios as a member of the Earth Orchestra and performing live at the British Museum, The Other Palace Musical Theatre, Pembroke Lodge, Stoke Rochford Hall, etc.
